JobsBot Commands!

JobsBot's default prefix is ++, but can be customized to whatever you want using the ++prefix command!

Jobs Information Commands

Command Alias(es) Permission Description
help - Everyone Shows all JobsBot commands & their descriptions.
itemupgrade iu / iupgrade Everyone Upgrades your item to the next level.
jobsitem ji Everyone Displays your job's item's level.
jobsjoin jj Everyone Joins the job you specify.
jobsleave jle Everyone Leaves your current job.
jobslist jobs Everyone Lists all joinable jobs.
jobsshop js Everyone Shows information/costs about leveling up job items.

Business Commands

Command Alias(es) Permission Description
businesscard bcard Business Members Displays your current business.
businesscreate bcreate Everyone Creates a business for $100,000.
businessdelete bdelete Business CEO Deletes your business.
businessdescription bdescription / bdesc Business CEO Sets business description.
businessfire bfire Business CEO/CFO Fires the mentioned user from your business.
businesshire bhire Business CEO/CFO/COO Hires the mentioned user to your business.
businessinvest binvest Business Members Invests money from your wallet into your business treasury.
businesspay bpay Business CEO/CFO Pays money from the treasury to a user.
businessquit bquit Business Members Leaves your business.
businesstype btype Business CEO Specify what your business does.
businessdonate bdonate Business Members Donates the specified amount of the specified item.
businesswarehouse bwarehouse / bwh Business Members Shows all donated items & product.
productcreate pcreate Business CEO Creates 1 product from 25,000 of each item in the warehouse.
businesslevel blevel Business CEO Lets you upgrade your business, if you have the required total products created.
